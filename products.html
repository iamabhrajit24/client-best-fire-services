Fire Extinguishers

ABC Dry Powder

Suitable for Class A, B, and C fires. Extinguishes fire by forming a barrier between the oxygen and fuel source.

  • Best Use: Factories, Warehouses, General Offices
  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 2kg, 4kg, 6kg, 9kg
  • ISI Certified

CO2 Type

Designed specifically for Class B (flammable liquid) and Class C (electrical) fires. Leaves no residue after discharge.

  • Best Use: Server Rooms, Electrical Panels, Labs
  • Available Sizes: 2kg, 4.5kg, 9kg (Trolley)
  • Seamless Cylinder
  • Safe for sensitive electronics

Mechanical Foam

Ideal for Class B liquid fires (petrol, diesel, oils). Forms an aqueous film that prevents oxygen supply and stops re-ignition.

  • Best Use: Chemical Plants, Petrol Pumps, Garages
  • Available Sizes: 9L, 45L
