Fire Safety Regulations for Industries in Daman & Silvassa

Navigating local compliances in the Union Territories of Daman, Diu, and Dadra & Nagar Haveli (Silvassa) can be challenging. A failed fire NOC (No Objection Certificate) audit can lead to massive fines or even temporary closure of your industrial operations.

The Essential Compliance Checklist

Industrial operations in this region are strictly governed by the National Building Code (NBC) of India, tailored with local fire department mandates. An average chemical or manufacturing facility requires:

  • ISI Certified Extinguishers: Standardized placement and valid hydro-testing records.
  • Fire Hydrant Rings: Dedicated water tanks purely for firefighting purposes, with functioning diesel/jockey pumps.
  • Signage & Exit Routes: Photo-luminescent "EXIT" signs and clear floor markings for assembly points.
  • Alarm Systems: Manually operated call points (MCPs) interspersed closely throughout the factory floor.

How to Prepare for the Audit

Auditors heavily scrutinize maintenance logs. Having an organized ledger showing monthly inspections by a certified third party (like Best Fire Services) immediately establishes good faith and compliance.
