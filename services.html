System Installation

Proper installation is critical for the effective functioning of fire safety equipment. A poorly placed extinguisher or an incorrectly wired alarm system can result in catastrophic failure during an emergency.

  • Strategic placement evaluating fire risk zones
  • Compliance with National Building Code (NBC) & BIS
  • Installation of Hydrants, Sprinklers, and Alarms
  • Clear signage setup

Maintenance & Refilling

Fire extinguishers need to be thoroughly checked and refilled either after usage or upon expiration of the extinguishing agent. We operate a state-of-the-art refilling facility in Vapi ensuring rapid turnaround times.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing of cylinders
  • High-quality ISI certified refilling agents
  • Replacement of damaged valves, hoses, and gauges
  • Detailed refilling certification label provided

Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

Don't leave fire safety to chance. Our comprehensive AMC guarantees that all your fire fighting equipment is proactively tested, maintained, and fully operational year-round.

  • Scheduled monthly/quarterly inspections
  • Detailed audit reports and inventory tracking
  • Priority emergency response for breakdowns
  • Guaranteed compliance for factory audits

Fire Safety Training

Equipment is useless if personnel do not know how to operate it safely. We offer hands-on training tailored for your facility’s specific hazards.

  • PASS (Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep) technique training
  • Live fire demonstration and practice
  • Evacuation protocols and mock drills
  • Certification for trained staff members
